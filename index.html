How it works

Build a lamp as a system.

Every LampForge design is composed of three interchangeable modules — mix, match, and print to create lighting that's entirely your own.

Base

The foundation of every lamp. Choose from weighted, slender, or wide-platform profiles — each engineered for stability and proportion. The base anchors the silhouette and sets the visual tone for the whole assembly.

Shade

The shade defines the character of the light — diffused or directed, wide or focused. Organic curves, geometric panels, and perforated patterns each cast a different mood. Print texture becomes part of the design, not a limitation.

Adapter

The adapter bridges your design to the real world. Every module ships with compatibility for LED strips, E27, and E14 fittings — so you can move from digital canvas to printed lamp without compromise or custom wiring.
